# StartSmart UAE — 2-Minute Demo Script

**Demo idea:** Camel milk tasting boxes for stargazing tourists

1. **Open StartSmart UAE.** "Founders in Al Qua'a have ideas but don't know the first step — or they spend money before knowing if anyone wants it. StartSmart UAE fixes both."
2. **Click the camel milk demo button**, choose customer type "Stargazing tourists", and click **Get my first step**.
3. **Judge Summary** — "Here's the whole pitch: problem, user, solution, impact, evidence, and why it's local."
4. **First Step Today** — "A specific action they can do tonight — no money, no license."
5. **Opportunity Score** — "An honest score out of 25, with a reason and an improvement tip for each part."
6. **Founder Decision** — "The app gives a clear verdict: run a small pilot."
7. **Proof Mode** — click **Fill demo feedback**. "The founder tests with 10 real people. We turn that into a live demand verdict — evidence before spending. They can export it as CSV too."
8. **What not to do** — "It actively warns against spending too early — the heart of Challenge 1."
9. **WhatsApp** — click **Copy**, then **Open WhatsApp**. "A ready message to test demand."
10. **Follow-up** — ask "How do I find my first customer?" "Local, specific answers."
11. **Download PDF** — "A clean founder report they can keep or print." Then click **العربية**: "And all of it works in Arabic, for their community."

**Close:** "StartSmart UAE — test your idea before you spend."

Keep it under 2 minutes. Practice the click path once before recording.
